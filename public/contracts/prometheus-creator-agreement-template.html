2. Definitions

  • "Referral" means any new user who signs up for Prometheus using the Creator's unique referral code or link.
  • "Qualifying Purchase" means any paid subscription purchased by a Referral through the Prometheus app.
  • "Net Revenue" means the gross purchase price minus applicable app store fees (typically 15-30%).
  • "Commission" means the percentage of Net Revenue payable to Creator for Qualifying Purchases.
  • "Content" means any promotional materials, posts, videos, or other media created by Creator mentioning Prometheus.

3.3 Payment Terms

  • Minimum Payout: $50 USD (balances below this threshold roll over to the next period)

4. Creator Obligations

4.1 Content Requirements

Creator agrees to:

  • Create authentic, honest content about Prometheus
  • Clearly disclose the partnership relationship in accordance with FTC guidelines and local regulations (e.g., #ad, #sponsored, #partner)
  • Not make false, misleading, or unsubstantiated claims about the app or its features
  • Not engage in any deceptive practices to generate referrals (fake accounts, bots, incentivized installs)

4.2 Brand Guidelines

Creator agrees to:

  • Use Prometheus branding assets only as provided and approved by Prometheus
  • Not modify, distort, or alter the Prometheus logo or brand elements
  • Maintain a professional image consistent with Prometheus brand values
  • Submit major promotional campaigns for approval before publication (optional but recommended)

4.3 Prohibited Activities

Creator shall NOT:

  • Use paid advertising that directly competes with Prometheus's own advertising
  • Bid on Prometheus brand terms in search advertising without written permission
  • Send unsolicited bulk messages or spam
  • Create content that is offensive, discriminatory, or harmful
  • Misrepresent the relationship with Prometheus (e.g., claiming to be an employee)

5. Company Obligations

Prometheus agrees to:

  • Provide Creator with a unique referral code and tracking link
  • Accurately track and report all Referrals and Qualifying Purchases
  • Pay commissions in accordance with Section 3
  • Provide access to a Creator Portal for real-time statistics and earnings
  • Notify Creator of any material changes to the app, pricing, or commission structure with at least 30 days notice
  • Provide marketing materials and brand assets upon request

7. Term and Termination

7.1 Term

This Agreement begins on the date of signature and continues for an initial term of 12 months, automatically renewing for successive 12-month periods unless terminated.

7.2 Termination Without Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with 30 days written notice to the other party.

7.3 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately if the other party:

  • Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 14 days of notice
  • Engages in fraud, illegal activity, or conduct harmful to the other party's reputation
  • Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

7.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:

  • Creator's referral code will be deactivated
  • Creator will receive final payment for all earned commissions within 45 days
  • Creator must cease using Prometheus branding and materials
  • Sections 6 (IP), 8 (Confidentiality), and 9 (Limitation of Liability) survive termination

8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any non-public information disclosed during this partnership, including but not limited to:

  • Commission rates and payment terms
  • Business strategies and unreleased features
  • User data and analytics
  • Financial information

This obligation survives termination for a period of 2 years.
